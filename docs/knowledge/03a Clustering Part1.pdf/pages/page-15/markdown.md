Euclidean distance

d (1, 2) = \sqrt {\left| 2 - 6 \right| ^ {2} + \left| 8 - 3 \right| ^ {2}} = \sqrt {4 1}

$$
d (1, 2) = | 2 - 6 | + | 8 - 3 | = 9
